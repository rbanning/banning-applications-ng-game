The Game

Introduction

We here at Banning Applications love games. We have developed two games to showcase our Angular skills... and because we really enjoy working on new, fun, projects.

During COVID lock down, we developed The Hunt where players (friends and family) could try their luck on a variety of mini-games. While the game was well-received, we learned that our real satisfaction came from all of the new code libraries and techniques we had to discover to achieve the desired effect/feature. For us, every new project is a learning experience and building games ("for the fun of it") is our Professional Development Program.

The first game - The Unsplash Game - asks you to group a set of photos by category. If you have played the wonderful game Red Herring , this game will be familiar. If you have not played Red Herring, why not? ;-)

The second game - The X-Letter Word Game - asks you to guess a word of a given length. Many of you are familiar with Wordle. We have three versions of the word guessing game:

  • The Three-Letter Word Game (We play this game during dinner, on car rides, and now online!)
  • The Four-Letter Word Game (Our family tried this (analog version) but went back to the 3-letter version.)
  • The Five-Letter Word Game (We just learn of Wordle and are addicted!)
